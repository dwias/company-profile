ABOUT

Jooyshipping Holdings Co., Ltd. is a leading shipping logistics company in intra-Asia area. Its business scope covers container liner shipping, ship management, ship brokering, international freight forwarding, LCL, project logistics, logistics delivery, customs broker, warehousing, container depot, port, shipping agency, bulk cargo, airlift, etc. At present, Shipping Group and Logistics Group are the two core divisions under Jooyshipping Holdings

Jooyshipping Group covers container liner shipping, ship owner business, ship management, ship brokers, container leasing and other related business. Until Jun 30, 2016, the company operates 74vessels, including 46 self-owned vessels. The existing 60 services routes covers 13 countries and regions, 59 major ports in area of China, Japan, Korea ,Taiwan, Hong Kong, Vietnam, Thailand, Philippines, Cambodia , Indonesia,Singaporeï¼ŒBrunei and Malaysia. According to Alphaliner statistics, counted by capacity, Jooyshipping ranks No. 22 in the global container shipping enterprises as of Jun 30, 2016. The container transportation volume in 2015 exceeded 2.163 million TEU(excluding None-charge containers).

Jooyshipping Logistics Group covers freight forwarding, customs broker, LCL, project logistics, logistics delivery, shipping agency, bulk cargo, warehousing, container depot, port, etc.We have a logistics park with the business of warehousing and depot in Qingdao, Shanghai of PRC , Vietnam Haiphong& Ho Chi Minh and Thailand Bangkok& Laem Chabang . Meanwhile, Jooyshipping Logistics has maintained a long-term cooperative relationship with global famous enterprises such as Damco Logistics, Itochu Logistics, Hanjin, Singamas, Tsingtao Beer etc.
