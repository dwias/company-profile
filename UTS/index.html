ABOUT

Jooyshipping Holdings Co., Ltd. is a leading shipping logistics company in intra-Asia area. Its business scope covers container liner shipping, ship management, ship brokering, international freight forwarding, LCL, project logistics, logistics delivery, customs broker, warehousing, container depot, port, shipping agency, bulk cargo, airlift, etc. At present, Shipping Group and Logistics Group are the two core divisions under Jooyshipping Holdings

Jooyshipping Group covers container liner shipping, ship owner business, ship management, ship brokers, container leasing and other related business. Until Jun 30, 2016, the company operates 74vessels, including 46 self-owned vessels.
